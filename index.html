The society we live in today revolves around cheap, easy, and fast. With those three values in mind, the decisions and actions humans have made have costed the environment so much. What can you do to live a more eco-friendly lifestyle?

What does it mean to live an eco-friendly lifestyle?

Many people today are aware of the large enviornmental issues caused by humans that afflict our planet, and make an effort to adopt a lifestyle that minimizes their individual impact (Environment Blog). This website strives to share the easiest ways to live a more environmentally friendly lifestyle.

Why would someone want to live an eco-friendly lifestyle?

There are many benefits to striving to live more environmentally friendly. For example, it can be more cost efficent, build a better community, promote a healthier lifestyle, and help raise awareness for future generations to come (Campbell).

Ready to Get Started?

Here are a few tips you can incorporate into your daily life right now, with little to no effort!
  1. Turn off all the lights and electronic devices when you leave the room
  2. Limit your shower to less than 10 minutes
  3. Take notes and make reminders on your phone instead of writing it down on paper
  4. Is it a nice sunny day? Hang your clothes outside or on racks inside to dry instead of using the dryer
  5. Use a reusable waterbottle instead of a plastic one
  6. Bring a reusable bag to the grocery store instead of using the plastic ones
